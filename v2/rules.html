Game Rules

Game Objective

    Goal: Conquer enemies' central base.

  • Once a central base is conquered, conquered troops have the following turn to recover.
  • If after this turn the base remains under control of the player that conquered it, then, in a more than 2 player game:
    • On a 3 or less he preserves the army and infrastructure.
    • On other cases, the troops losing their home base are removed from the board.
    • IPCs of the loosing player passes to the conqueror up to its maximum possible IPC cap.
  • In a two player game the game is over.

Setup

    Map:
  • Recomendation is to play in a hexagonal grid of 20 hex height and 40 hex width
  • Map size to be at least 5 by 5 A4 sheet in landcape mode.
  • Map generation open source tool by Azgaar (simply awesome)

    • Each player choses a starting location at least 15 tiles apart of each other.

    Army setup
    Place a Base Complex in each home tile.
    The initial army of each player is 4 infantry and 50 IPC.

    Start the turn as normal.

Tiles and Unit movement

  • Any given tile can hold up to 10 between infrastructure and units per player
  • Units or equipment being transported are not counted.
  • For an island to be usable as land must be the size of at least half of a tile.
  • Air units must end its movement in an Air Base, Aircra Carrier or Refueler. If not the unit is destroyed, except refuelers.
  • Movement of units cannot be split in parts. E.g. a transport with movement 4 cannot move 2 tile wait for units to be loaded and then move again.
  • Except for ICBM, any unit passing through a contested tile ends the movement.

Infrastructure

    Infrastructure can be built only by player infantry units. Each unit can build once infrastructure per turn and only where it is located. Since happens in different phases, building does not exhaust unit movement.

    Facilities

    • Base Complex: It is the Base Complex of the player.
      They come with defense against ICBM.
      On ICBM attack roll three dice, on one 3 or less, the missile is destroyed before attack
      If the missile hits the complex it counts as one hit
    • Financial Complex: It increse production for units.
      For 50 IPC one of these can be swapped with the Base Complex.
    • Refinery: Each refinery has oil production value equal to the value of a roll of one die when build on land, and two dice when built on water (as if it were two refineries). Each turn it roll corresponding dice.
      If production in a refinery was not collected in the previous turn, change to the higher value.
      Put value markers in each refinery to state production of the turn.
      When oil is loaded onto a transport, move the marker to the corresponding transporter unit
      A Refinery accespts only one transporter at a time.
      Can be built on land or sea but there must be 3 tiles separating it from any other Refinery.
      If built on a tile with water and land, it is always built on land.
    • Industrial Complex These are for production of units and equipment.
      For production to occur, it must be connected by road to the destination base.
      Although ships are sea units they must still be produced in industrial complexes connected by road to a Naval base.
      They can hold up to three units, these units cannot move, attack nor defend.
    • Military Base: Is the starting base for land units.
    • Air Bases: Can hold a max of 5 planes.
    • Naval Bases: Can hold a max of 2 ships docked. Docking is required to repair ships
    • ICBM Silo: Can hold 1 ICBM.
  • Complexes requires 2 hits to be destroyed; one hit disables it, the next hit, if not fixed, destroys it.
    If there are units in the complex when it is destroyed, they are also destroyed.
  • Complexes can be attacekd as if they were land units.
  • Disabled infrastructure cannot produce, fix, lunch or receive units of any kind.
    In the case of Air bases, the planes must be sent to other bases in the range of movement of the plane or are considered destroyed.
  • Disabled units only can be moved; planes cannot land nor take off from them.
  • For storage at complexes, two packs of equipment count as one unit
  • Repairs of any complex or unit cost half of its construction cost.
  • Complexes can be repaired in contested tiles, but cannot build new ones unless the tile is friendly.
  • Refineries in contested tiles keep producing as long as they are not disabled

    • Infrastructure

  • Roads: Roads allow production to reach the destination base.
    Roads are not conquered, they are either destroyed or remains usabble.
  • Pipes:Pipes can be used by refineries to pump oil to Base Complex.
    When a pipe line is built from a Refinery to Base Complex, the Refinery is considered to be connected to Base Complex and production is available to the player in the every turn.
    A pipe line in a tile count as one unit. So it can be attacked and destroyed.
    Pipelines are not conquered, they are either destroyed of keeps passing oil.
    Pipes can be built in tiles with friendly units.
    Each refinery must have its onw line of pipes.
  • Bridges

      A bridge can not be built in tiles with enemy units on them.
      The max lenght of a bridge is three sections of road.
      Each bridge section counts as one tile in regards to movement same with every side of land.
      Units on a bridge cannot defend.
      Bridges can be attacked like any other infrastructure (not individual sections of the bridge).
      Destroying a bridge destroy units on the bridge.
      Only submarines and transports can pass by water under bridges.

    • These are destroyed with one hit.

Units

    • Infantry:Infantry is not produced, it is directly bought and can be placed at any tile with friendly units or complexes.
    • Transports Can transport Oil or units or equipment.
      Mechanized units can transport one unit of oil or pack of equipment.
      Sea transport can transport 3 units of oil or 3 units between land units and pack of equipment.
      Air transport can transport 3 between land units and pack of equipment.
    • Air transports can land with troops on an Aircraft Carrier, yet troops must be unloaded at an Air base.
    • Sea transport when the ship in on shore the oil can be loaded onto a mechanized infantery in the same territory or to Base Complex if located on the same tile.
    • BattleshipRequires 2 hits to be destroyed, one hit disables it. Has to dock at a Naval Base to be fixed. Repair cost is half the ship costs.
    • Aircraft Carrier: They are not transpornts, they act as naval bases. Can hold up to 3 air units. Requires 2 hits to be destroyed, one hit disables it. Has to dock at a Naval Base to be fixed. Repair cost is half the ship costs.
    • AAA: Have an auto defence system that allows them to perform an defensive engage on passing by planes, ICBM or surface ships at shore on enemy turn, once per turn. Use attacking stats.
    • Submarines: Have an auto defence system that allows them to perform an defensive engage on passing by ships on enemy turn, once per turn. Use attacking stats.
    • ICBM: Can attack any tile (works like strategic bomber attack).
      • Must move at least three tiles in the same direction before changing direction.
      • On a 5 or less the attack succeeds. On a 1 the impact is in the tile and all surroundings.
      • Accounts for any number of hits required to destroy what is on the tile.
    • Refueler Refueling implies restore the full range for the plane.
        • Can refuel one air unit in the same territory. A plane (except refuelers) can be refueled only once per turn. The unit can complete its movement at the refueler in which case in cannot defend if attacked and other planes cannot refuel at the refueler.
  • Units transporting or being transported cannot attack or defend.
  • If a ship is docked it cannot defend if the territory is attacked
    If the tile is conquered, docked ship is captured
  • Units in storage, docked or parked count towards the limit of units per tile.

Equipment


Equipment is all kind of unit that needs to be transporded and can either enhance another unit or be placed on a tile.

When unit or tile is equipped, the range of attack of the equipment is the range of movement of said equipment

    Long Range Missiles
  • Can be equipped onto Tactical Bombers.
  • A Long Range Missile is consumed after use.
  • As with planes can overfly territory with enemy units
  • It is affected by preemptive attack
  • One pack consist of two missiles

    • Torpedo
  • Can be equipped onto submarines or destroyers.
  • A torpedo can be used to attack any ship.
  • A torpedo is consumed after use.
  • As with ships cannot overpass territory with enemy units
  • It is not affected by preemptive attack
  • One pack consist of two torpedo

    • Landmine
  • Can be placed on a tile.
  • One pack consist of 2 landmine
  • Attacks units passing by the tile.
  • landmine are consumed if mine attack succeds.

    • Seamine
  • Can be placed on a tile.
  • One pack consist of 2 seamines
  • Attacks units passing by the tile.
  • Seamine are consumed mine attack succeds.

    • Swarm drone
  • Can be placed on a tile.
  • One pack consist of 1 swarm drone
  • Attacks units in range of the drone.
  • Swarm drone is consumed after use


A pack of equipment count as one unit for storage or transport.
  • A unit can be equiped only at the corresponding base of the unit

    • IPC Collection


      Player can choose to roll dice for IPC collection for each refinery or one die for the Base Complex.
      If rolling for the Base Complex on a 2 or less earns 5 IPCs. IPC is collected every time a unit of oil reaches the Base Complex.

    • Oil must be moved from Refineris either by land, sea or combination of both to the Base Complex to be converted into production IPCs.
      Each transport can load from one Refinery at a time. To load or unload oil the movement must end in the corresponding complex.
      Refineries connected to Base Complex through a pipe line collect production without the need of transports.
      Conversion value is 1 to 1 the value of the dice of the Refinery to IPC.
    • Collected IPC accumulates to what player already has, up to a maximum of 50 IPC.

    Production

    • Base Complex allows for 20 IPC of production.
    • Each Financial Complex accounts for 20 IPC of additional production per turn. All IPCs of production from all complexes is summed up to determine the total production of the player for the turn.
    • Production of units cannot be split across multiple turns.
    • Refinery production does not exhaust

      • Units and equipment are produced in Industrial Complexes.
      When produced they are automatically transported by roads connected to the destination base.
      If there are no roads, the unit is temporarily stored on the Industrial Complex.
      Up to three units can wait on Industrial Complex for transport.
      If more units are produced, the units must be transported or discarded.
      • Land units must be transported to a Military Base.
      • Airplanes must be transported to an Air Base.
      • Ships must be transported to a Naval Base.
      • ICBM must be transported to an ICBM Silo.

      Everything is in play from the next moving phase of the turn in which it is bought or produced.

    Combat

      Combat is resolved after all players moved, so order of contested tiles is not important. Combat is resolved rolling one D6 for each atacking and defending unit. Each unit has an attack and defence value depending of the unit type.
      Either attack or defence is a hit if the die roll value for the unit is equal or less of the value for the unit type.
      As units move, if there are enemy units in the destination tile, combat occurs.
      Each tile can be attacked only once per turn.
    • Resolving combat:
      From attacking units chose up to 5 units to run the attack.
      From defending units, chose up to 5 units to run the defence.
      These are the only units that will combat this turn.
      All other units remain in the tail as backup units.
      Roll one die per chosen attacking and defending unit. Each unit accounts damage according its type.
      Both attacking and defending damage is acounted.
      At the end of attack there is no frontline or backup units. All units are in the same group.
    • The player causing damage choose what unit or complex get the damage. If damage surpasses frontline units, damage can go to backup units in the tile according to attacking unit rules (effective agains in stats page applies to all damage allocation).
    • If the tile has constructions, they can also be chosen as a target instead of units.

      • After the attack remaning units stays in the tile except for airplanes that must return to an airbase or refueler, the tile remains contested.
      Ownership of contested tiles do not change until only one player has units in the tile at which point he owns the tile.
      Infrastruce is fully operative (including pipes, roads and bridges) in contested tiles unles it receives damage.
      When a unit is equiped the equipment moving range is attacking range of such equipment
      Infantry bought cannot be placed in constested tiles.

      Attack of equiptment is resolved at the end of the movement phase of the player that is moving. When the attaked is a base with units, if able such units leave the base and can move in its turn if able.

      Preemptive-attack does not wait unitil combat phase. It can happen as many times as the conditions are met during the same movement phase even for the same unit. The only restriction is each unit with preemptive-attack can only be used once per occurrence.
      A preemptive-attack is neutralized if attacking army also has at least one unit with preemptive-attack. In this case the preemptive-attack does not happen.

    Turn

    • Update oil production at Refineries.
        Each player rolls one die per each refinery and allocate production at payer choice.
        Markers of production can be placed face up or down.
    • Buy units and build/relocate infrastructure
        • Buy units up to the max production IPC. All units must be placed in the corresponding complex.
        Infantry can be placed in any tile with friendly units or complexes.
        Buy infrastructure and place in map, tiles must have friendly units.
        Relocate infrastructure paying the relocation cost.
    • Move units
        • Decide playing order for moving units.
        Each player roll a die, the lower goes first.
        Move as many units as wanted. Each unit can move only once per turn.
        If retreating from a contested tile, the destination must be friendly non contested. Retreating units cannot move through the edge enemies come from.
        Units can move to contested tiles.
        If the destination tile is occupied by enemy units, combat is declared.

        At no time the tile must contains units of a player exceeding the maximum allowed per tile.
        Units at a Refinery might choose to collect oil or stay as defending units.
        To keep track of attackers at contested tiles, place a counter for the attacking player in the tile.

    • Resolve combats
        • Roll dice for attackers and defenders.
        If no enemy units in the tile, the player conquers the tile and its remaining infrastructure.
        When combat finishes air units must use its remaining movement points to reach an airbase
      Collection
        All players collect production from transports with oil at Base Complex or through pipelines.

    Proof of concepts

      Developments (under development ...)

        Every turn a player can spend up to 10 IPC in development. Only one development can be undergoing at a time.
        A completion dice is rolled every turn after spending in development reaches 30 IPC. A development is complete when the completion dice roll is 2 or less.
        An extra completion dice can be bought with 10 IPC.

        • RadarRadars work in conjuction with AAA. Radars can detect air units passing by its adjascent tiles and extend AAA pre-attack to thier surronding tiles.
        • Stealth technologyOnce developed radar cannot detect air units
        • Portable AAAAAA can be installed in Battleship and Aircraft Carriers
        • DronesWorks like short range ICBMs
        • Paratroopers: Infantry can be dropped in any tile that is not under combat.
        • Land units level upLand units can be damaged by units with same or higher level. Before rolling the completion dice, a level up dice must be roll with a value higher that the current level.
        • Air units level upAir units can be damaged by units with same or higher level. Before rolling the completion dice, a level up dice must be roll with a value higher that the current level.
        • Sea units level upSea units can be damaged by units with same or higher level. Before rolling the completion dice, a level up dice must be roll with a value higher that the current level.
